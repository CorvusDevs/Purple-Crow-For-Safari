Description:
Twitch Plus brings the full third-party emote experience to Safari — BetterTTV, FrankerFaceZ, and 7TV emotes render directly in chat, just like on other browsers.

But emotes are just the beginning. Twitch Plus includes 50+ features designed to make Twitch genuinely better:

EMOTES & CHAT
- Full BetterTTV, FrankerFaceZ, and 7TV emote support
- Real-time emote updates when streamers add new emotes
- Split chat with 11 color themes for easier reading
- Emote tab-completion in the chat input
- Chat timestamps, spoiler tags, and keyword highlights
- Spam and bot message filters
- Searchable chat history
- User pronouns display

AUTOMATION
- Auto-claim channel points, Drops, Moments, and watch streaks
- Set once and never miss a reward again

VIEWING
- One-click Picture-in-Picture
- Anonymous viewing mode — watch without appearing in the viewer list
- Auto theater mode with pure-black OLED backgrounds
- Transparent chat overlay for cinematic viewing
- Force any video quality from 160p to Source
- Quality persists across channel navigation

TOOLS
- Screenshot and clip download
- Stream uptime display
- Follow/unfollow age tracking
- Channel point balance in the tab title

PRIVACY & PERFORMANCE
- Zero data collection — no analytics, no tracking, no accounts
- All settings stored locally on your device
- Native Safari extension — not a Chrome port
- Lightweight with minimal resource usage

Fully localized in 19 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Italian, Russian, Dutch, Turkish, Arabic, Thai, Polish, Swedish, Indonesian.
