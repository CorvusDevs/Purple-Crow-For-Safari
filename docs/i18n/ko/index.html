Features

Everything Twitch and Kick should have built in

Third-Party Emotes

Full BetterTTV, FrankerFaceZ, and 7TV support with real-time updates. See every emote your favorite streamers use.

Split Chat

Alternating message backgrounds with 11 color themes. Read fast-moving chats without losing your place.

Auto-Claim

Automatically collects channel points, Drops, Moments, and watch streaks. Never miss a reward again.

Picture-in-Picture

One-click PiP button right in the player. Keep watching while you browse, work, or do anything else.

Anonymous Viewing

Watch streams without appearing in the viewer list. Your presence stays completely hidden.

Theater & OLED Mode

Auto theater mode with pure-black OLED backgrounds and transparent chat overlay. Cinematic viewing experience.

Chat Tools

Timestamps, spoiler tags, emote tab-completion, spam filters, keyword highlights, and searchable chat history.

Quality Control

Force any video quality from 160p to Source. Persists across channel navigation, set it once and forget.

19 Languages

Fully localized in English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Chinese, and 11 more.

Tab-Complete Carousel

Type a partial emote name, press Tab to cycle through inline previews of the next 6 to 10 candidates. See what you're picking before you commit.

Mention Highlights & Sound

A subtle tone, a title flash, and color-coded mention rows when someone calls your name. Customizable per channel.

First-Time Chatter Highlights

Spot brand-new viewers instantly with a colored row background. Great for streamers welcoming newcomers in real time.

Deleted Message Display

Deleted messages stay visible as strikethrough text instead of disappearing. The conversation keeps its context.

User Pronouns

PronounDB integration shows each chatter's preferred pronouns inline beside their name. Opt-in, privacy-respecting.

Custom Nicknames

Set custom display names for any user, per channel. The original username stays available on hover for moderation.

Enhanced User Cards

Click any username for a richer card with follow date, recent messages, channel badges, and a quick-jump to their channel.

How it works

Up and running in seconds

1

Install from the App Store

Download Purple Crow from the Mac App Store. It's a lightweight Safari extension.

2

Enable in Safari

Open Safari Settings, go to Extensions, and enable Purple Crow. Grant permission for twitch.tv.

3

Enjoy Twitch

Visit twitch.tv and everything works automatically. Emotes load, points get claimed, chat gets better.

Why Purple Crow

Built for Safari users

The emotes you're missing

Thousands of community emotes from BTTV, FFZ, and 7TV are invisible in Safari. When chat spams a reaction and you just see text, that's the problem Purple Crow solves.

50+ quality-of-life features

From auto-claiming channel points to split chat, PiP, anonymous viewing, screenshots, clip downloads, and spam filters, features that make Twitch genuinely better to use.

Native Safari extension

Not a Chrome extension ported to Safari. Purple Crow is built specifically for Safari with native performance, no background processes, and zero data collection.

Requires macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later. Works with Safari 17+.
